Core Positioning

Hotel BFF is a category-creation play, not a demand-capture play. Most hotels don't know these compliance deadlines exist, and those that do don't know where to start. The messaging needs to educate before it sells.

Positioning Statement (Draft) Hotel BFF is the end-to-end sustainability platform that helps hotels prepare for mandatory compliance deadlines — from self-assessment through certification — without disrupting operations or requiring massive upfront investment.

Messaging Principles

Educate Before You Sell

Most hotels don't know 2030 deadlines exist. Content must lead with the regulatory timeline and what it means for their business — then position Hotel BFF as the solution. This is the same approach that worked for LiaFi (category education, not product pushing).

Compliance as Opportunity, Not Threat

Frame sustainability compliance as a competitive advantage, not a burden. Hotels that act early get ahead of competitors who will scramble at the deadline. Staff retraining delivers immediate cost savings before any infrastructure investment.

Trust Through Credentials

CB Ramkumar's GSTC Trainer status is a credibility anchor. The zero-commission vendor marketplace builds trust (they make money from the platform subscription, not from vendor kickbacks). Lean on these trust signals in all content.

Start Small, Scale Later

Hotels fear sustainability = expensive overhaul. The message should be: start with the free self-evaluation, get a score, train your staff (immediate ROI), then work toward certification at your own pace. Remove the "all or nothing" perception.
