The Hotel BFF Journey (5 Stages)

Hotel BFF frames their product as a journey with five stages. The acronym spells out a progression: Ascertain, Train, Obtain, Attain, Maintain. This is their core product narrative.

The 5-Stage Journey to Certification
#StageWhat It IsStatusDetails
1AscertainFree self-evaluation against 42 GSTC criteriaLiveFree. ~40 min to complete. 7-day validity. Google Form-based.
2TrainVideo training program with case studies, puzzles, gamesLiveSelf-paced. ~90 min. 30-day validity.
3ObtainVendor marketplace for sustainability products and servicesLiveZero commission. Location-based. ~8 weeks to implement.
4AttainPre-audit certification guidance using GSTC-accredited bodyLiveTemplates, documentation, guidelines. ~4 weeks.

Brand Voice (from current site)

Current Tone Casual, self-aware, slightly irreverent. Tagline: "Put your Best Foot Forward." Opens with "OMG, NOT ANOTHER WEBSITE ABOUT SUSTAINABILITY" — acknowledges buzzword fatigue head-on. Frames sustainability as a business advantage (save money, better search results, attract new-gen travelers), not just an ethical obligation. The "BFF" name itself signals approachability.
This voice will be evaluated and potentially refined during Phase 1 brand discovery. It may or may not survive the rebrand — but understanding where they are today helps us guide where they should go.
